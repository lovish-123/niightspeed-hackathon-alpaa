MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act): This is a flagship Indian labor program that guarantees 100 days of wage employment to rural households. It aims to enhance the livelihood security of people in rural areas. Skill India: Under Skill India, several schemes and programs have been initiated to empower the workforce with the skills needed for various industries. The Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is one such program. Pradhan Mantri Rozgar Yojana (PMRY): It is a credit-linked subsidy program that provides financial assistance to people who want to start their own enterprises. Startup India: This program encourages entrepreneurship and offers various incentives and support for startups, which can eventually create employment opportunities. Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP): This credit-linked subsidy program provides financial support for setting up micro-enterprises and small businesses, thereby promoting self-employment. National Career Service (NCS): NCS is a platform that connects job seekers and employers and provides information on job openings, career counseling, and skill development. Skill Development Schemes: Various state and central government skill development programs aim to train individuals in various skills, making them more employable. Atal Pension Yojana (APY): APY is a pension scheme that provides pension benefits to the unorganized workforce. Stand Up India: This program promotes entrepreneurship among women and SC/ST communities by providing financial assistance for starting new ventures. Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY) and Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY): These are insurance schemes that provide accidental death and disability cover, and life insurance coverage, respectively.
